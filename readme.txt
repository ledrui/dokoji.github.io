Appsperia

'Appsperia' is a One Page HTML template featuring a unique layout to showcase your application or website. 
Basically you can use your keyboard to scroll between sections that correlate with the imagery within the alongside device. 
Once in a section with multiple items, for example Testimonials, you can scroll left and right. 
There are 4 different demos (food, creative, travel, game) for you to check out, each with dark and light schemes including video background options. 
252 sales and a 4.5 star rating means you are looked after in the support department too. 
I like how 'KeyDesign' theme shop from Romania have steered away from the norm and bring us their take on how to showcase an app.
(Source: https://onepagelove.com/appsperia)
